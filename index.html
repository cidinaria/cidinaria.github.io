About me

I am a scientist and researcher specializing in Bioinformatics, focused on transforming complex data into real advancements for healthcare and science. Holding both a Master’s and a PhD in the field, I currently work as a bioinformatician at Barretos Cancer Hospital, where I apply Machine Learning and transcriptomic analysis to discover oncology biomarkers. Outside the lab, I find balance by swapping code lines for creativity in the kitchen, testing new recipes always accompanied by a good cup of coffee. I also love hitting the road to travel and explore new destinations, or simply relaxing at home binge-watching a great series.
